<Our Mission>

"Our mission is to provide a study and work place optimized for coders, web developers, and others who primarily work remotely from their laptops. The rustic style of our café gives for a cozy feel, but don't be mistaken. Our free wi-fi, printing stations, and an abundance of power outlets make Coder's Café the perfect tech-friendly environment for you to work, study, or simply enjoy a delicious cup of our award-winning Coffee."

<Our Coffee>

"Our Coffee is riveting, intriguing, marvelous, and mind-blowingly awesome. Hailed from the depths of Atlantis, our beans are notably the most unique in the universe. We use an unprecedented brewing preparation process never before conceived in all of human history. First, we wash our beans and soak them in unicorn tears. Then, we rinse them off and place them directly into a jar in the windowsill looking out at the city. We leave them there until they develop big city dreams. Finally, we give them a pep talk and send them on their way to the shops where they are made into magical cups of coffee greatness."
